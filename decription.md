Elementor Layout Recreation Guide: BestOnwardTicket.com
This guide details the layout structure of the BestOnwardTicket.com homepage for recreation in Elementor.

Global Style Estimates
Primary Color: Tech Blue (e.g., #0073e6) implies trust and travel.
Background: Clean White (#ffffff) or very light gray (#f9f9f9) for sections.
Typography: Clean Sans-Serif (e.g., Robot, Open Sans, or Poppins).

1. Header / Hero Section
   Purpose: Immediate value proposition and primary action (Booking).

Section Layout: Two Columns (50% / 50%) or Single Column centered if the form is horizontal.
Observation: Most "Onward Ticket" sites feature a strong headline on the left and a booking form on the right/center.
Column 1 (Left - Content):
Widget: Heading (H1)
Content: "Rent A Proof Of Onward Travel Or Visa Application"
Widget: Heading (H2/H3)
Content: "for only $14" (Highlight the price, possibly different color).
Widget: Text Editor
Content: "Valid for 48 hours ++. We never store your credit card information."
Widget: Button (Optional secondary CTA)
Content: "Contact Us" or "Learn More".
Column 2 (Right - Action):
Widget: HTML or Shortcode (The Booking Form)
Note: The original site uses a specific booking engine (ID #rb-booking-form). In Elementor, use the Form widget (Elementor Pro) or a shortcode from a booking plugin like Gravity Forms or a specialized travel plugin.
Fields needed:
Trip Type (One Way / Round Trip)
From (Airport)
To (Airport)
Departure Date
Passenger Name / Email 2. Trust & Features Bar
Purpose: Credibility markers directly below the hero.

Section Layout: 3 or 4 Columns.
Settings: Background color #f9f9f9 (light gray) to distinguish from Hero.
Widgets (Repeated per column):
Widget: Icon Box (Icon + Title + Description)
Item 1: "Legitimate" (Icon: Checkmark/Shield). Description: "Verifiable PNR".
Item 2: "Instant" (Icon: Lightning/Time). Description: "Received in minutes".
Item 3: "Secure Payment" (Icon: Lock/PayPal Logo). Description: "Secure PayPal checkout". 3. "How It Works" Section
Purpose: Explain the 3-step process.

Section Layout: Single Column (Header) + Inner Section (3 Columns).
Header:
Widget: Heading (H2, Centered). Content: "How It Works".
Inner Section (3 Columns):
Column 1:
Widget: Icon Box (Position: Top).
Icon: User/Edit (Input).
Title: "1. ENTER INFORMATION".
Description: "Choose your route, airports, date, and passengers..."
Column 2:
Widget: Icon Box.
Icon: Plane/Credit Card.
Title: "2. CHOOSE FLIGHT & PAY".
Description: "Select flight, enter details, pay with PayPal."
Column 3:
Widget: Icon Box.
Icon: Download/File.
Title: "3. GET TICKET INSTANTLY".
Description: "Download ticket instantly + email copy." 4. Testimonials ("See What Our Users Say")
Purpose: Social Proof.

Section Layout: Single Column.
Widgets:
Widget: Heading (H2, Centered). Content: "See What Our Users Say".
Widget: Testimonials Carousel (Elementor Pro) OR Reviews widget.
Configuration: 3 Slides per view, Loop enabled.
Content:
Mateo: "Amazing service! ... ticket was 100% as we needed it!"
Byron Watts: "PHENOMENAL! ... worked with me when I had TWO airplane cancellations."
Dustin Allister: "Used them for years... refund in 30 minutes."
Alex Sasha: "10 min later i received my ticket... Would have been stuck."
Widget: Button (Optional). Content: "See more reviews on Trustpilot". 5. SEO / Info Section ("Why Use Us?")
Purpose: Detailed explanation for SEO and skeptical users.

Section Layout: 2 Columns (Text + Image) or Single Column (Text-heavy).
Column 1:
Widget: Heading (H3). Content: "Why Use Us?".
Widget: Text Editor.
Content: Long text block explaining "Onward travel is becoming common...", "Bus ticket used to be enough...", "Accepted at airport check-ins", "Perfect for visa applications".
Column 2 (Optional Side Visual):
Widget: Image. Content: Image of a passport, plane ticket, or happy traveler to break up text. 6. FAQ Section
Purpose: Address common objections.

Section Layout: Single Column (narrower width, e.g., 800px) for readability.
Widgets:
Widget: Heading (H2, Centered). Content: "Frequently Asked Questions".
Widget: Accordion or Toggle.
Item 1: "What exactly am I purchasing?" -> "Genuine reserved flight itinerary..."
Item 2: "Is this legitimate for visas?" -> "Yes. Every reservation is made through GDS..."
Item 3: "How can I verify?" -> "CheckMyTrip or airline site..."
Item 4: "Difference from fake ticket?" -> "Real PNR in system..."
Item 5: "Can I board?" -> "No." 7. Footer
Purpose: Contact and Links.

Section Layout: 3 Columns.
Column 1: Logo + Short blurb.
Column 2: Links (Home, How it works, Reviews, FAQ).
Column 3: Contact.
Widget: Icon List.
Items: Email ([email protected]), Facebook Link.
Bottom Bar: Copyright text.
